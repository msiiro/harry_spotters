# Welcome to the Vanishing Cabinet!

This repo is for the deployment of the Vanishing Cabinet, a web app for the tracking of fan fic you read with your friends.

## Key Features

- Upload directly from AO3, ePub files, or manually by entering in fanfic information
- Track your progress from want to read, to reading, to read (or dropped)
- Pull books directly into your shelf from others that your friends are reading or have read
- Rate titles and add custom tags and reviews

### Disclaimer
The webpage is currently in Beta, and not available to the public.
